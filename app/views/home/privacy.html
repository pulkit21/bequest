INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect information to provide better services to all of our users, and we collect that information in the following ways:

INFORMATION YOU GIVE US

We collect personal information from you when you register for a Bequest Life account and apply for insurance products and services through our Site. For example, when you register for a Bequest Life account, we’ll ask you for your name and e-mail address, and when you apply for insurance products and services, we’ll ask you for your date of birth, social security number, address, phone number, driver’s license number, employment, financial, family and housing information, existing life and health insurance coverage information, activity information, health information, bank account and/or credit card information, and other information. INFORMATION WE COLLECT FROM THIRD PARTIES

When you register for a Bequest Life account and/or apply for the insurance products and services offered through our Site, we will collect information from third parties to, among other things, verify your identity; assist in the underwriting of your application; make eligibility, risk rating, policy issuance and enrollment determinations; obtain reinsurance; administer coverage; administer claims; protect against fraud; and conduct other legally permissible activities. In addition, if another company refers you to us, we may collect your information from that company in order to process your application for the insurance products and services offered through our Site. We generally collect your identifying information (for example, your name, address, e-mail, telephone number, date of birth, social security number, and employment information), bank account information, financial information, health information, driving record, criminal history, activity information, and other personal information from these third parties. You agree that by using our Site, services and products, you are granting us the right, power, and authority to act on your behalf to access and transmit your personal and financial information from your financial institution.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT BASED ON YOUR ACTIVITY ON OUR SITE

In order to provide our services to you, improve our business and maintain records required by law, we collect information about your transactions and activity. For example, we collect information about your computer and your visits to the Site, such as your IP address, geographical location, browser type, referral source, length of visit, button clicks, mouse movement and page views. In addition, we collect device-specific information such as hardware model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, mobile network information including phone number, and information about your payment history.

We and our marketing partners and service providers use technologies such as cookies, beacons, tags, and scripts, to analyze trends, administer our Site, track users’ movements around our Site, and gather demographic information about our user base as a whole. We may receive reports on this activity on an individual and aggregated basis. We use cookies when you sign in to your Bequest Life account to keep track of your personal session, help authenticate your account, and detect fraud. Cookies are also used to gather statistical data, such as which pages are frequently visited, what is downloaded, and the address of sites visited immediately before or after coming to our Site. You can control the use of cookies within your web browser. However, if you reject cookies, your ability to use some features or areas of our Site may be limited.

We may collect and store information (including personal information) locally on your device using mechanisms such as browser web storage. Various browsers may offer their own management tools for removing browser web storage.

We also partner with third parties to manage our advertising on other websites and evaluate the overall performance of our Site. Our third party partners may use technologies such as cookies to gather information about your activities on our Site and other sites in order to provide you advertising based upon your browsing activities and interests or help us track the success of our marketing efforts or overall Site performance.

Although we do our best to honor the privacy preferences of our users, because we believe that there is a consistent industry standard for how to respond to Do Not Track browser settings, we are not able to process or respond to such signals from your browser at this time.

If you do not wish to receive these communications please click on the “unsubscribe link” provided in such communications.
